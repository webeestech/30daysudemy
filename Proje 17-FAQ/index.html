A Connect licence enables you to access multiple online services from your vehicle such as Online Traffic Information, Weather and News.

In your myCar app, scroll down to Functions on Demand and tap Go To Shop. The available licences will be shown along with the features which they will unlock on your car.

Wifi hotspot enables you to connect up to 8 devices to the internet from the comfort of your car, enabling you to stay connected with friends and family, enjoy games and entertainment, and conduct important business. In order to enable this feature, a data package must be purchased from our partner Next Telecom.
